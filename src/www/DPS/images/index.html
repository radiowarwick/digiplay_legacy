RaW

With continuing support from STA Travel and IBM, RaW is enjoying another memorable year of student radio, broadcasting the great music, info and entertainment you need to soundtrack your life at Warwick.

Highlights in Term 3 include our annual holiday competiton thanks to STA Traval, stay tuned to RaW to find out who will win. We will also be keeping you going through your revision with some good music and chat. Take a look at the schedule to see what takes your fancy.
